What Grab It does
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for Salesforce OmniStudio developers who are tired of writing reports from memory.

🔍
Auto-Detection

Grab It automatically detects the OmniScript, DataMapper, FlexCard, or Integration Procedure you're working on — name, version, and description included.

✍️
Smart Auto-Fill

The description field is pre-filled from the component's own description. Just verify, tweak if needed, and hit Save. No more typing from scratch.

📊
Flexible Reports

Generate Daily, Weekly, or Monthly work reports with one click. Copy to clipboard and paste anywhere — Google Sheets, Word, email, or your company system.

✂️
Highlight & Grab

Working in Google Docs or any web page? Highlight any text, click Grab It, and the selected text is instantly captured as your task description.

🔒
100% Private

All your data stays in your browser. Nothing is sent to any external server. Your work logs are yours and yours alone.

🗂️
Data Management

Filter logs by month, edit or delete entries, export backups, and clean up old data — all from a simple, clean interface right in your browser toolbar.

Simple by design
Four steps.
That's it.

Grab It is designed to get out of your way and let you focus on building.

01
Open a component

Navigate to any OmniScript, DataMapper, FlexCard, or Integration Procedure in your Salesforce org.

02
Click Grab It

Click the Grab It icon in your Chrome toolbar. The component name and description are automatically detected and filled in.

03
Save your task

Verify the story number and description, make any edits you need, and hit Save Task. Done in seconds.

04
Generate your report

At the end of the day, week, or month — go to the Report tab, select your period, and copy your formatted report instantly.

Component detection
Knows what you're
working on.

Grab It intelligently detects four core OmniStudio component types and captures all the relevant information automatically.

⚡

OmniScript

Captures Type, SubType, Version, Language, and Description directly from the designer header. Also detects the current Step you're working on.

📊

DataMapper

Detects the DataMapper name and version from the designer header. Opens the Settings panel to capture the full description.

🃏

FlexCard

Grabs the FlexCard name and version from the designer toolbar and captures the description from the Setup panel on the right.

🔗

Integration Procedure

Detects the Integration Procedure name and version from the header and captures the description from the Properties panel.
